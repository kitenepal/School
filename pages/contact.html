About Us

MIA (Mamti International Acedemy) is well equipped and peace environmental school for children. Here the teacher and management are young and energetic with supervision of social well recognized personnel. All the teachers and management are well energetic and educated with different skills and ideas for the society. "Youngsters are the pillars of the nation", so we are presenting the well known school in the peacefull environment with affordable fees for everyone. Our main target is to facilitate for all status of students with good quality of educatiuon with social well being and make capable to tackle the upcomming obstacles in our daily life.
